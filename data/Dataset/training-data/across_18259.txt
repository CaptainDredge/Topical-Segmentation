FRANKFURT  (Reuters) - Porsche SE's ( PSHG_p.DE ) embattled chief executive, Wendelin Wiedeking, is leaving the company, Germany's business weekly WirtschaftsWoche reported on Wednesday, without citing sources.According to the magazine, a successor will be decided on shortly.
