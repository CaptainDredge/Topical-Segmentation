British Gas lost around 250,000 customers early in the year, but turned the losses around by cutting prices in March and again in April, by a total 20 percent for gas and 17 percent for electricity. This contrasts with last year when it lost over 1 million customers amid rising gas prices and customer service issues.

SINGAPORE  (Reuters) - Barclays Capital stood by its forecast of $76 a barrel for average U.S. crude in 2009, saying improving demand and a supply slide will combine to buoy oil prices.The forecast, at more than $17 above a consensus $58.48 in the most recent Reuters poll, was among the highest in the survey of 30 analysts.
