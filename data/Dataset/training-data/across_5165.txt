SYDNEY  (Reuters) - BHP Billiton Ltd/Plc ( BHP.AX ) ( BLT.L ) on Thursday took its case for a takeover of rival Rio Tinto Ltd/Plc ( RIO.AX ) ( RIO.L ) to well-heeled investors on Thursday, saying a marriage could better capture markets in fast-industrializing Asian economies.Chief Executive Marius Kloppers told 400 high net worth clients of UBS at a meeting in Sydney that the around $170 billion takeover of Rio would see the merged entity control 20 percent of China's iron ore market and make it a bigger force in coal, copper and other commodities sought after in China.
