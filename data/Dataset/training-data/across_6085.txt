SAN FRANCISCO  (Reuters) - Apple Inc reported record sales of Mac computers and strong growth overseas, but iPhone shipments were slightly below some of Wall Street's most bullish forecasts.The company also reported a sharply better gross margin as it benefited from more favorable component costs and higher revenue. Apple's stock edged higher in after-hours trading, after closing up 2.7 percent on Nasdaq before the news.
